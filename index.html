What is Bora Bora?

Known as the Pacific Pearl, it's an amazing island with idyllic and delightful landscapes, crystaline waters and a beautiful and unique variety of flora and fauna.

This island is located on the South Pacific Ocean and belongs to one of the five archipielagoes of the French Polynesia, called Society Islands.

Bora Bora is an atoll, which is an island with lagoon, and it¬¥s surrounded by islets.
